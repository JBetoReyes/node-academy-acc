Exercise 2

Requirements Edit your logger module to meet the next requirements
  • Add headers to your logs
  • The new logs should not override the previous ones
  • Use moment.js and moment-timezone to give the following format to the date of your logs: YYYY-MM-DD HH:mm:SS UTC

Your logs should look similar than the following:

> log.txt ``` Date Level Message 2019-05-25 15:04:59 UTC ERROR Error Message 2019-06-19 11:04:59 UTC WARN This is a warning ```
